Introducing Kirat-Coin, a new digital asset created out of curiosity for the Web3 space. Kirat-Coin was developed by me, with inspiration and guidance from Harkirat Singh, who introduced me to the exciting world of Web3.

Kirat-Coin is a token built on the Solana blockchain. Solana is the perfect platform for experimenting with new digital assets like Kirat-Coin.

As a demo coin, it has no practical use or value, and is purely a learning and experimentation tool. However, it demonstrates some of the basic concepts of creating a token on the blockchain, including the ability to transfer and track ownership of digital assets in a decentralized and trustless way.

In creating this Coin, I have gained valuable experience in the Web3 space, and learned about the potential for blockchain technology to revolutionize many aspects of our lives.

I hope you find it as interesting and exciting as I do!
